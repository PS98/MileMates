About MileMates

MileMates as the name suggests, "Mates for Miles". We are available for help at your doorsteps. We believe in serving you the best way possible by means of your vehicle. We take great pride in servicing our customer in the best way possible. In today’s busy and quick moving world, everyone wants to save his/her time and energy so that they could invest the same in other productive work. So it is quite common that our vehicles don’t get the attention they need (repair/service/maintenance etc).

We, at milemates.com are determined to make your vehicle maintenance haslefree through an online platform. We bring all authorized service centres under a roof, enabling customers to pick from the best service centres available in the market. The idea is to reduce the waiting time in the service centres and provide a hassle free service booking, management and maintenance of vehicles for people.

Our Services

We personally faced these problems related to servicing of vehicles and felt their is a need for a simplified and convenient online system. Issues like, hectic work hours, difficulty in locating nearby service centres, sudden breakdown in traffic are faced by many people in day to day life, and we are here to provide solutions for your basic vehicle problems.
