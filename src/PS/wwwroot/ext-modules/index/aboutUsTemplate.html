About MileMates

MileMates as the name suggests, "Your Mates at every Mile", is a company which cares about your vehicle as a friend. Our company is based on the belief that our customer's needs are of the utmost important to us. Our entire team is committed to meeting those needs. As a result, a high percentage of our business is coming from repeat customers and word of mouth.

We would welcome the opportunity to earn your trust through our services and deliver you the best service in the industry.

We believe in serving you the best way possible by means of your vehicle. We take great pride in serving our customer in the best way possible. In today’s busy and quick moving world, everyone wants to save his/her time and energy so they could invest the same in other productive work. It is quite common that our vehicles don’t get the attention they need (repair/service/maintenance).

We, at milemates.com, are determined to make your vehicle maintenance hassle free through our reliable online platform. We bring all authorised service centres under a roof, enabling customers to pick from the best service centres available in the market. The idea is to reduce the waiting time in the service centres and provide a hassle free service booking, management and maintenance of vehicles for customer.
